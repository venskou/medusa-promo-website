Medusa Coin Roadmap

We are excited at the additional value that the Medusa concept can bring to the crypto industry. We have just begun our project and have many exciting developments on the way. Below are our plans for the road ahead.

November 2017

Demand established, market study completed, company formed.

January 2018

Consultants engaged and they hold initial meetings with card schemes and acquirers to identify the Medusa solution.

February 2018

Ultra high value payment solution defined, Core team of advisors are engaged.

March 2018

Commercial plans finalised. Initial legal reviews. Processes clarified and escrow partners engaged.

April 2018

Banks agree to ultra high trading deposits. Insurance policies confirmed.

May 2018

Medusa token created. Wallets enabled and cold storage ready.

June 2018

Draft White Paper released and circulated to advisors for comments.

July 2018

Final legal checks, final White Paper. Website and social media presence begins. Marketing partnerships engaged.

August 2018

Token sale begins, client waiting list begins.

December 2018

Agent network in full rollout. Medusa service ready for trading.
