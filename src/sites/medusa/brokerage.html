Brokerage

Medusa Brokerage is an invite only trading platform where high value tokenized asset transactions are facilitated in an OTC trading environment. Basic membership for clients is free of charge and involves the standard sign up process crypto users have become accustomed to when joining exchanges.

After passing a strong KYC/AML process the client can begin trading similarly to traditional platforms by inputting orders for transactions of listed crypto tokens or ICOs.

In addition, the client can also speak directly to Medusa Brokers who are available to help originate, find counterparties to and manage OTC orders for the buying and selling of any tokenized asset.

Trading Platform

The client facing Medusa Brokerage Platform strives for simplicity in order to provide an instantly usable interface even for members who have no prior experience with using trading platforms.

The platform breaks down into three main sections as above:

  1. 1. Standardized token assets, which are assets that are well known and ubiquitous such as listed cryptocurrencies and ICO tokens.
  2. 2. Bespoke tokenized assets, which are any assets that have be denominated in Medusa and listed on the platform for sale.
  3. 3. Services, where members can gain immediate access to remittances or Medusa Brokers
Our goal is to operate one of the largest and most expansive high volume tokenized asset brokerages in the world.

Asset Tokenization

We have realized from the beginning that our unique payment facility coupled with the brokerage allows us to apply the concept of simple and safe high value payments globally to any asset class.

To this end we have created a process whereby we are able to tokenize any asset to make it eligible for transacting through our platform to the benefit and convenience of our clients.

Asset Tokenization Process

Any asset can be tokenized, but in the case pertaining to Medusa, high value items, for example gold, intellectual property, a house, a business, or a painting can be tokenised according to the following steps.

  • 1
    • Medusa Brokerage client selects their desired asset to be tokenized so it can be listed for sale on the platform.
    • a. Asset description, valuation and terms of delivery are all included in the documentation which will form a part of the offering.
    • b. Desired number of tokens to denominate the asset is selected by owner.
    • c. Exit preference is selected to inform Medusa whether the desired asset to acquire after the conclusion of the transaction is fiat, Medusa tokens or an alternative asset.
  • 2
    • Medusa produces the standard Medusa trust for the seller which provides the legal authority to transfer ownership of the seller’s asset, their house by way of example, into a legal entity called a Medusa trust.
    • a. Beneficial Owner is a legal term where specific property rights, such as use and title, belong to a person even though legal title of the property belongs to another entity.
    • b. The trust is designated to legally own what was originally the seller’s asset, in our example the house, but the beneficial ownership remains with the seller.
    • c. The trust document determines the rules that govern how the trust works and the Medusa trustee is appointed, whose function is to ensure that the rights of the beneficial owners of the trust are represented according to the trust’s rules.
  • 3
    • Simultaneously to the establishment of the trust, the virtual asset is divided by the desired amount of tokens selected by the seller. The requisite number of asset tokens are created and now have a unit value. The trust document declares that whoever holds those tokens is deemed by the trust to be the beneficial owner of the asset.
    • a. For example, the seller’s house could be denominated in 100 HOUSE tokens, 10,000 HOUSE tokens or just 10 HOUSE tokens, depending on what the seller has defined and hence appears in the trust document.
    • b. The tokens represent beneficial ownership of the assets held by the trust. Each token can be given specific voting rights or any other rights which the trust document specifies.

After this process has occurred, the seller’s house is now fully owned by the trust but the beneficial owners, in other words the person or people who are to receive all benefits of the trust’s asset, will be the holders of the tokens.

Initially the holder of all the tokens which represent the asset, and therefore the full beneficial ownership of the house, was the seller. The seller could still enjoy the asset, use it according to whatever rules were originally put in place in the trust at its inception, but the seller no longer legally owns the asset.

It is clear to see that once this process is completed the beneficial ownership of the house can easily be traded from person to person for any other asset at any time, with the added benefit of visibility which a public blockchain provides given that there is an absolute and perfectly clear audit chain and clarity on the trustees.

HOUSE tokens can be exchanged for another property, fiat, crypto or any other tokenized asset listed for exchange on the platform through the intermediation of Medusa tokens.

Investors purchasing all, or any fraction of, the token supply will become owners of a corresponding percentage of the asset those tokens represent. In the event the asset appreciate or produce returns the investors will benefit. Provided the terms of the trust allow, token holders may also be vested with voting rights to participate in the governance of the asset.

There is no limit to the flexibility of this solution and we have come to realize this during our testing phase as we have been approached by prospective clients who wished to use our platform to buy and sell yachts, art, real estate and other exotic assets or to send remittances internationally at fraction of the cost of existing service providers.
