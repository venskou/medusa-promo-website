Insurance

Mitigating risk in not only important for institutions but also customers. Naked Period

We have coined this term to describe the position an investor occupies when undergoing a tradition token purchase in the market. In the event a bank transfer is used to attempt to purchase a desired crypto currency the buyer has to send monies to an exchange. At this point the investor has sent 䢴at which is now in transit to the exchange. The buyer therefore does not have possession of the 䢴at money nor the crypto currency. Therefore we call this period, where the buyer does not have ownership of either asset type, the Naked Period. With MPS the purchase of Medusa tokens is instantaneous and the Naked Period in effect does not exist.

Intermediary Risk

Unbeknownst to many market participants, money transfers often happen through several intermediary banks to arrive at their destinations. The journey of a funds transfer can be a lot more complicated then what people are led to believe. The interception or stoppage of a wire can occur at any point within the chain of institutions involved in the execution of the order. Because the buyer purchases Medusa tokens through our MPS facility upon card authorization immediately, and those Medusa tokens are accepted by all sellers on the Medusa platform for their assets, Medusa has managed to bypass all intermediaries, hence the risk of a transaction being stopped, delayed or intercepted for any reason are eliminated.

Exit Option

Providing liquidity is not a one way street. Whilst MPS can be used for investors to buy crypto currencies ICOs or tokenized asset at high volumes for 䢴at currencies, the need to unwind crypto positions for 䢴at also exists. ICOs that have successfully raised crypto to fund their project will have a need to convert those coins into 䢴at to fund operations. Medusa provides the option to unwind positions and have 䢴at currency pushed onto the nominated card of the seller.

Exit Limit

The same principals of limits apply for the exiting of positions as for the purchases of all assets on the Medusa platform. Medusa aims to start with a $2.5m purchase and sell limit and increase this rapidly as we scale. As a service provider our aim is to allow our members to conduct their trading in the simplest manner possible and have the fewest number of limitations and restriction imposed on them. Our belief is that the elimination of limits and risks will result in the increasing of investment in and wider adoption of crypto currencies.

Medusa aims to charge an average of just 3.5% to the purchaser of any assets by card, making it the cheapest solution on the market for large scale investors.

Medusa Brokerage understands the risks investors are facing in the markets. Not only price risk of the acquired asset, but the process of sending the funds to their destination as well as the performance of the counterparty upon the completion of the transfer all pose substantial risks.

Undoubtedly the accumulated risks from the potential break points of such investments have posed too large of an obstacle to surmount for many individuals sitting on the side-lines of the crypto market. We at Medusa have created a platform where members need only focus on investment decisions as we have stripped out these risk factors for transaction conducted through MPS.

Each purchase conducted through Medusa Payment Service is automatically insured up to $2.5m for all Medusa Brokerage members as standard. There is no premium charged for insurance and there are no exceptions with respect to asset type.

Any transactions that exceed the $2.5m threshold will also benefit from insurance. However these transactions will be requested from our insurers prior to the commencement of the trade and will be executed through an accredited Medusa Broker.

The full details of the thresholds for tranches and ultra-high transactions will be disclosed at the launch of the payment gateway.
