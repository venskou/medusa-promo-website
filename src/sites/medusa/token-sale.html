In Greek mythology, Medusa was a monster, a Gorgon, generally described as a winged humanoid female with living venomous snakes in place of hair. Gazers upon her hideous face would turn to stone.

In classical antiquity the image of the head of Medusa appeared in the evil-averting device known as the Gorgoneion.. Olympian deities, Athena and Zeus, both are said to have worn the gorgoneion as a protective pendant.

Medusa was beheaded by the hero Perseus, who thereafter used her head, which retained its ability to turn onlookers to stone, as a weapon until he gave it to the goddess Athena to place on her shield.

Since that time, and through the ages the use of Medusa’s image has become a symbol which protects the bearer from his enemies, and for the same reason a good luck charm.

Abstract

Medusa brokerage is a unique high value crypto currency payment service provider that makes buying, selling and managing large volumes of ICOs or established tokens simple for the first time.

Medusa provides its clients the ability to acquire tokens with no crypto experience or skills so that their entry into the industry can be seamless and risk free.

The problem

Purchasing large volumes of crypto currencies is a difficult, slow and risky process to undertake and this barrier has prevented many investors from entering the market.

The industry is constantly associated with limits imposed by third parties which costs investors both time and money, especially when dealing with a volatile market where opportunities disappear quickly.

The solution

Medusa technology eliminates these barriers by enabling clients, whether individuals or corporations, to buy large volume of crypto currencies with their existing bank card.

The brokerage offers a portfolio of upcoming ICOs as well as a selection of established coins for sale in volumes not previously seen in the industry and insures each transaction.

Staking Reward

The Medusa Staking Reward calculator displays the annual staking reward.

A maximum of 4% over the staked tokens will be paid to your wallet annually.

The total amount of Medusa tokens in the staking wallet determines the maximum amount of tokens you can receive.

The price of Medusa tokens changes constantly due to trading in the open market.

The annual staking reward is calculated by: Total amount of Medusa tokens * 0.04

* The 4% staking reward is calculated over a one year period. Stakes are not evenly distributed over the year. A period such as a day/week/month can be different from others. Over a one year period you will earn a 4% stake reward when staking.
