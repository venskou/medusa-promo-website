Medusa Agent Program

Medusa Agents can now turn their natural ability to network and build contacts in the real world into a legitimate profession that will earn them considerable income if they are successful in accomplishing their main two main tasks: recruiting clients and other agents.

Having analysed this space we understand how potential deals emerge. Individuals come across interested parties who are looking for a counterparty to a particular deal. A good agent then begins the process of sourcing leads through their existing network. It is this exact network of key individuals which we wish to help agents monetize in a structured and standardized way.

Currently the market is replete with individuals who are vying for contacts and deals in the extremely competitive OTC markets. In numerous asset classes buyers and sellers are willing to complete deals, except the uncertainty in the intermediary space prevents genuine parties from transacting. Medusa is reshaping this space by guaranteeing certainty with respect to commissions and legitimacy with the Medusa brand and concept. By providing the appropriate motivations and guarantees, the OTC market will be enriched with a new approach to doing business.

The agent network is an extremely valuable asset to Medusa and its clients, as it is a self-motivated team of professionals dedicated to the growth of the Medusa ecosystem. In recognition of the contribution agents make to the long term success of the platform we have enabled them to profit from their work in a way not previously seen in the market.

The Two Main Activities

Agents have two potential portfolio building activities they can undertake: recruiting clients and other agents. Let us look at both of these options in detail.

  • 1. Agent recruitment is the act of introducing another active agent to Medusa Brokerage for the purposes of helping that individual become accredited and with their help increase the potential reach of the collective network to additional clients.
  • 2. Client recruitment is the act of bringing a client from the preferred portfolio of target clientele to Medusa Brokerage for the purpose of helping the client create an account and use the unique services offered to them.

Building a Portfolio

By pursuing their two main objectives, a successful agent will invariable accumulate an expanding portfolio of agents and associated clients in their network. This expansion is a natural consequence as Medusa Brokerage is an invite only platform. All subsequent clients that arrive to the platform within the network of an agent have the potential to generate financial benefits.

The compensation scheme is founded on the concept of Degrees of Separation (DoS). This simply refers to the number of people by which a person within your network is separated from you.

As you are the originating point, an Agent who is DoS 2 relative to you is a person who has been introduced to the platform by an agent you referred personally. DoS 7 would be an individual who is the seventh person in a line of subsequent agent referrals from you.

This structure clearly creates a multi tired system that orders agents around you as your network expands. Medusa Brokerage tracks this network through eight degrees of separation and displays it to all agents to show the current size and reach of their portfolio.

Medusa understands that its not always “What you know” but “Who you know” that matters.

Now that the concept of the network of agents has been clearly defined, it is easy to visualize this structure.

Each node on this specific network represents an agent. As described previously, besides recruiting other agents the chief task, naturally, is to recruit clients. Therefore, any and all clients that are associated with an agent within your network fall within the same DoS as their respective agent. For example if an agent on DoS 3 has three clients then those three clients are also on DoS 3 in relation to you.

Eligibility of Comissions

There is a lot of competition in the OTC market between agents to claim clients and commissions. It is very important to apply a clear set of rules to help determine which agent is eligible for commissions on a transaction.

In addition there is a significant amount of payment insecurity for agents as traditionally they were not covered contractually. This risk prevents many OTC agents from connecting leads for fear of being cut out of the deal. Medusa Brokerage eliminates this fear and empowers agents to push forward and build a profitable client portfolio.

The following are the rules with respect to determining eligibility for commissions:

  • 1
    • One agent brings a new client to Medusa
    • a. Client is a new member and recognizes agent who introduced him to the platform
  • 2
    • Several agents bring new client to Medusa
    • a. Client recognizes the agent that introduced him to the platform
    • b. Agent submits all subsequent individuals in the contact chain for client sign of
  • 3
    • Client finds Medusa Brokerage without agents
    • a. Agent is assigned to account at the discretion of Medusa Brokerage

This means that the agents within the brokerage operate like bridges between the clients, who are nodes in a network. The objective of the agents is to create as many interlocking connections as possible. Clients have the authority to execute trades through their account and a connection between nodes is a synonym for a completed transaction.

Earnings from your Network

All commissions are paid in Medusa tokens to agents directly to their Medusa Brokerage wallet upon settlement of any transaction that warranted a monetary reward to the appropriate individual.

Medusa Bokerage pays out 50% of its transaction revenues in commissions after card processing expenses have been deducted. This means that in the event of a transaction that has $6,000 margin after card fees on the buy side, $3,000 is Available for Distribution (AfD) for all buy side agents. Out of the total AfD available to the buy side agents, $1,000 is paid to the Connected Agent and $1,000 to the Connecting Agent if applicable.

In the event that there are multiple intermediary Connecting Agents they split the available commission equally.

The remaining 33% of AfD or $1,000 is paid out to the Agent Network. In the event there is no Connecting Agent(s), the available 33% is added to the Agent Network’s 33% for a total of 66% to be paid out to the network.
